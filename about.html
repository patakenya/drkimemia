About Dr Kimemia

Learn about our mission to empower individuals with knowledge and resources to prevent and manage non-communicable diseases like heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, and stroke.

Who We Are

Founded by Dr. John Kimemia, a renowned cardiologist with over 20 years of experience, HeartWise is dedicated to raising awareness about non-communicable diseases (NCDs). Our initiative began in 2010 with a vision to provide accessible, evidence-based information to help individuals prevent and manage heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, and stroke.

Our Mission & Vision

We strive to transform lives through education, prevention, and empowerment, creating a healthier world free from the burden of NCDs.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, accessible, and actionable information on NCDs, enabling individuals to make informed decisions about their health and prevent life-threatening conditions through education and early intervention.

Our Vision

A world where NCDs are significantly reduced through widespread awareness, proactive prevention, and equitable access to health resources, fostering vibrant and healthy communities.
